# AnkiDeutsch-App
Flip. Learn. Accelerate.

## About AnkiDeutsch 🇩🇪🚀

**AnkiDeutsch** is a specialized, hybrid flashcard application designed to help learners rapidly accelerate their German vocabulary and master noun articles (*der, die, das*). 

Unlike generic language apps, **AnkiDeutsch** gives you complete control over your learning journey. It combines the high-efficiency principles of spaced repetition with a laser focus on German grammar dynamics.

### Core Features

* **Custom Flashcard Creation:** Quickly input your own vocabulary words, precise translations, and contextual meanings.
* **Dedicated Article Tracking:** Built-in slots specifically for *der*, *die*, and *das* to build muscle memory for noun genders.
* **Accelerated Revision:** Smart review sessions designed to test you right before you are about to forget a word.
* **Hybrid UX:** Intuitive English functionality wrapped around essential German language structures for seamless navigation.

### Why AnkiDeutsch?

In German, learning a word without its article is only doing half the job. **AnkiDeutsch** ensures that every time you revise a noun, its gender is hardcoded into your memory alongside its meaning, helping you speak and write fluently, faster.
